Welcome to the Data Structures Lab

What We Do

We teach students practical and theoretical knowledge of data structures through hands-on lab exercises. Our mission is to foster a deeper understanding of algorithms and data management techniques that are essential for solving real-world problems.

Lab Curriculum

  • Week 1: Introduction to Data Structures
  • Week 2: Arrays and Linked Lists
  • Week 3: Stacks and Queues
  • Week 4: Trees and Graphs
  • Week 5: Sorting Algorithms
  • Week 6: Searching Algorithms
  • Week 7: Advanced Data Structures
