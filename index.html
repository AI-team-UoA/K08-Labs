About

This webpage concerns the Labarotories of the K08 Data Structures and Programming Techniques undergraduate course of the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications of the University of Athens for .

The goal of the Labs is to help students practice developing Data Structures that have been taught in the course's lectures. In addition, we showcase and use a variety of programming tools and techniques.

Lab Curriculum

  • Lab 0: Introductions and Git
  • Lab 1: C Recap
  • Lab 2: Modules/Unit tests/Makefiles & Linked-lists
  • Lab 3: Vectors
  • Lab 4: Valgrind
  • Lab 5: Binary Search Trees
  • Lab 6: Hashtables
  • Lab 7: Bloom Filters
  • Bonus: Generic Data Structures in C and C++
